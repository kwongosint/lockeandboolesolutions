Compliance & Ethics

Methodology,
not advocacy.

We operate strictly within the boundaries of public records, FOIA mechanisms, and lawful open-source intelligence. These are not aspirational statements. They are operational constraints.

Our Ethical Framework

Six principles we
do not compromise.

I
Public Record Only
Every piece of information we collect and report is drawn from publicly accessible sources — Secretary of State portals, county records, EDGAR, PACER, FOIA responses, and similar. We do not access private systems, accounts, or communications.
II
No Surveillance
We do not conduct physical surveillance of individuals, install tracking software, intercept communications, or engage in any investigative activity that requires in-person observation of a subject's movements or activities.
III
Cite Everything
Every factual claim in every Deliverable we produce carries a cited public source. If we cannot source a claim from the public record, we do not include it. This applies without exception, regardless of how confident we are in a finding.
IV
Data Only — No Advice
We provide data. We do not provide legal advice, investment advice, strategic recommendations, or advocacy. Our role ends at the delivery of accurate, sourced information. What the client does with it is their professional judgment to make.
V
No Political Advocacy
Locke & Boole does not engage in political advocacy, lobbying, partisan activity, or any work designed to influence electoral outcomes, legislative processes, or regulatory rulemaking. We do not take sides.
VI
Client Screening
We reserve the right to decline any engagement — and exercise it regularly. We do not accept matters where the intended use of our Deliverables appears designed to facilitate harm, harassment, illegal discrimination, or any activity that violates applicable law.

Legal Basis

Our OSINT
boundaries.

All information gathering by Locke & Boole Solutions LLC is conducted within the following legal frameworks. These are not guidelines — they are operational boundaries we do not cross.

Where we are uncertain whether a specific data collection method falls within these boundaries in a particular jurisdiction, we default to not collecting the data rather than assuming permissibility.

Applicable Frameworks
FOIA
State and federal Freedom of Information Act request mechanisms. We submit formal written requests and accept only lawfully produced records in response.
OSINT
Open-source intelligence gathered exclusively from publicly accessible sources — no password-protected, subscription-only, or privately held databases unless the Client holds a lawful subscription and grants access.
CFAA
We fully comply with the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act. We do not access any computer system, database, or network without explicit authorization from the system owner.
SCA
We comply fully with the Stored Communications Act. We do not attempt to access stored electronic communications, accounts, or private messaging systems under any circumstances.
FCRA
We do not conduct consumer reports as defined by the Fair Credit Reporting Act. Our services are not designed for, and may not be used for, employment screening, tenant screening, or consumer credit evaluation.
