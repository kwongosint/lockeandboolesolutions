Sectors Served

Built for those who
cannot afford to be wrong.

We serve legal, corporate, and government entities that require absolute precision in asset verification and structural intelligence.

01
Sector
Legal & Litigation

High-stakes legal proceedings demand a factual foundation that can withstand the full scrutiny of opposing counsel, depositions, and judicial review. Our deliverables are built for this environment from the first draft.

  • Boutique Family Law High-net-worth divorce and trust litigation where assets have been moved into holding structures to obscure marital value.
  • White-Collar Defense Mapping prosecution data to build counter-narratives and locate discrepancies in the government's ownership theory.
  • Bankruptcy Trustees Locating hidden assets transferred to related entities or nominee holders for creditor recovery.
  • Commercial Litigation Tracing judgment-debtor assets across state lines before a judgment becomes uncollectible.
02
Sector
Corporate & Finance

In M&A, private equity, and family office contexts, the ownership structure you see in the data room is rarely the complete picture. We find what isn't disclosed — before you close.

  • Private Equity & Hedge Funds Pre-acquisition shell mapping to avoid inheriting hidden liabilities, encumbered assets, or undisclosed co-owners.
  • Family Offices Auditing internal financial managers and advisors for unauthorized side-LLCs, conflicts of interest, or self-dealing structures.
  • Commercial Real Estate Verifying the ultimate human owner behind a layered entity holding a lucrative land parcel before committing capital.
  • Vendor Due Diligence Confirming that a prospective vendor is a legitimate operating entity and not a front, pass-through, or undisclosed affiliate.
03
Sector
Government-Adjacent

For defense contractors, journalism organizations, and compliance teams operating in the orbit of government, the integrity of the supply chain and source structures is a matter of both security and credibility.

  • Defense Contractors Ensuring that supply chain vendors are not front entities for adversarial actors, sanctioned parties, or prohibited counterparties.
  • Investigative Journalism Bulk data provisioning for large-scale accountability reporting projects requiring verifiable, independently citable sourcing.
  • Compliance & Ethics Teams Continuous partner monitoring to surface third-party restructuring before it becomes a FCPA, OFAC, or regulatory exposure.
  • Government Contractors Subcontractor and teaming partner verification to satisfy procurement integrity requirements.
If your matter involves ownership you cannot verify, we are the right firm. If it doesn't, we will tell you plainly.
