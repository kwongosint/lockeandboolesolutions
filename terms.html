Section 01

Definitions

For purposes of these Terms, the following definitions apply:

  • "Services" refers to all data intelligence, corporate structure investigation, asset tracing, beneficial ownership mapping, continuous monitoring, and training services provided by the Firm.
  • "Deliverables" refers to all reports, visual ownership charts, affidavits, executive summaries, monitoring alerts, training materials, and other outputs produced by the Firm in connection with a Client engagement.
  • "Engagement Letter" refers to the written document issued by the Firm specifying the scope, fee, and timeline of a particular engagement.
  • "Retainer Agreement" refers to the signed agreement establishing an ongoing subscription or VIP retainer relationship between the Client and the Firm.
  • "Matter" refers to the specific investigation, monitoring arrangement, or training engagement that is the subject of the Firm's work for a Client.
Section 02

Nature of Services

Locke & Boole Solutions LLC is a data intelligence firm. We are not a law firm. We do not provide legal advice, legal counsel, strategic advocacy, or any service that constitutes the practice of law in any jurisdiction.

All Deliverables produced by the Firm are provided for informational purposes only. Clients are solely responsible for interpreting Deliverables in accordance with their own legal obligations and in consultation with their retained legal counsel.

No attorney-client relationship, fiduciary relationship, or any other professional relationship beyond that of a data intelligence vendor and its client is created by engagement with Locke & Boole Solutions LLC.

The Firm's services are limited to the collection, analysis, and presentation of publicly available information obtained through lawful means, including Secretary of State filings, UCC records, EDGAR filings, county property records, federal procurement data, bankruptcy dockets, FOIA responses, and other public sources.

Section 03

Engagement & Retainer

No engagement is established until the Client has received and countersigned a written Engagement Letter or Retainer Agreement issued by the Firm, and until all applicable fees have cleared. Submission of an intake inquiry does not constitute engagement and creates no obligation on the part of either party.

The Firm reserves the right to decline any inquiry at its sole discretion, without explanation or obligation. We do not accept every matter. If we determine we cannot add material value to your matter, we will inform you promptly.

Subscription services (The Firewall, The Silent Partner) operate on a month-to-month basis unless otherwise specified in the Retainer Agreement. Either party may terminate a subscription with 30 days' written notice, subject to the terms of Section 9 (Termination).

Data & Deliverables

All Deliverables produced by the Firm are the property of the Client upon full payment of all applicable fees. The Firm retains no continuing rights to Deliverables following delivery and payment, except as necessary to fulfill ongoing monitoring obligations under subscription engagements.

Deliverables are based exclusively on publicly available information available as of the date of investigation. The Firm makes no representation that Deliverables reflect the complete or current state of any entity, asset, or ownership structure. Corporate structures change; public records have publication lags; and offshore registries vary in accessibility and reliability.

Clients are prohibited from representing Deliverables as having been produced by any entity other than Locke & Boole Solutions LLC in any judicial, regulatory, or public proceeding without prior written consent from the Firm.

Confidentiality

The Firm will maintain strict confidentiality regarding all Client identities, matter details, and Deliverables. We do not disclose Client relationships, matter specifics, or Deliverable contents to any third party except as required by valid legal process.

Clients agree to maintain the confidentiality of any non-public information shared by the Firm in connection with an engagement, including methodology documentation, proprietary workflow details, and analyst identities.

The Firm may reference aggregate, anonymized operational metrics (such as total assets traced, total entities monitored) for marketing and credibility purposes, provided no Client or matter is identifiable from such references.

Prohibited Uses

Payment Terms

Fees are non-refundable once work has commenced. If the Firm is unable to complete a project due to circumstances within its control, a pro-rata refund will be issued for incomplete work.

All fees are quoted and payable in U.S. dollars. Applicable taxes are the responsibility of the Client.
