Who We Are

Locke & Boole Solutions LLC is a boutique intelligence firm based in Florida, specializing in the verification of corporate structures and the mapping of hidden asset networks. We serve legal professionals, corporate finance teams, and government-adjacent organizations that require absolute precision in ownership verification.

We are not a law firm. We do not provide legal counsel. We provide data — verified, mapped, and delivered in subpoena-ready formats — and we let our clients do what they do best with it.

The Problem We Solve

In complex litigation, high-stakes mergers and acquisitions, and cross-border compliance matters, the surface-level corporate registry rarely tells the full story. Beneficial ownership is obscured through layers of shell entities, holding companies, nominee directors, and offshore jurisdictions deliberately designed to frustrate inquiry.

Most due diligence processes treat entity searches as a box to check. We treat them as the beginning of the investigation — because the most consequential information is never in the first layer.

Our Approach

We operate on a strict data-first philosophy. We do not rely on proprietary black-box databases. We rely on the public record — Secretary of State filings, UCC liens, EDGAR disclosures, county property records, federal procurement registries, and bankruptcy dockets — and we cite every source.

Every finding we produce can be independently verified. That is not a marketing claim. It is a structural feature of our methodology, and it is what makes our deliverables defensible in court.

Why Continuous Monitoring

Most intelligence firms deliver a report and move on. We do not. Our signature advantage is structural monitoring: after we map your partners, we keep watching. Registered agent changes. New UCC liens. Entity dissolutions. Quiet restructuring of liability shields. We flag these the moment they hit the public record — before they become your problem.

A partner who quietly restructured their corporate protection while you were focused on the deal is a liability you didn't know you were inheriting. The Firewall exists to prevent that.

Jurisdiction & Registration

Locke & Boole Solutions LLC is a registered Florida Limited Liability Company. All client engagements are governed by Florida law unless otherwise specified in a signed retainer agreement. We operate strictly within the boundaries of public records access, FOIA requests, and legally permissible open-source intelligence collection.

Our Principles

How we think about this work.

I
Data First
We do not begin with conclusions. We begin with filings. Every finding emerges from the public record, not from intuition or assumption.
II
Cite Everything
Every claim in every report we produce carries a footnoted source. If we cannot source it, we do not include it. This is non-negotiable.
III
No Advocacy
We provide data. We do not interpret, advocate, or take positions. Our clients are qualified to draw their own conclusions — that is why they hired us.
IV
Strict OSINT Boundaries
We operate exclusively within public records, FOIA mechanisms, and legally permissible OSINT collection. We do not conduct surveillance, trespass, or intercept communications.
V
Subpoena-Ready by Default
Our methodology is designed with the courtroom in mind. Every report we produce can withstand scrutiny — from opposing counsel, from judges, and from independent auditors.
VI
Security Without Exception
Client identity, matter details, and all deliverables are handled with encrypted communications and strict compartmentalization. We do not disclose client relationships.

Locke & Boole was founded by analysts who came from backgrounds in corporate compliance, legal investigation, and open-source intelligence. We are a small team by design. We take a limited number of engagements at any given time and we do not delegate critical work to junior contractors. Every report that leaves this firm has been reviewed by a senior analyst.
