Timely intelligence
on structural risk.

Briefings are short-form intelligence memos produced by the Locke & Boole analyst team when a development in corporate law, OSINT practice, or regulatory compliance warrants immediate attention from our clients.

What Are Briefings

Not analysis.
Intelligence.

Unlike our Insights pieces — which cover methodology and framework in depth — Briefings are short, direct, and time-sensitive. They are written when something changes in the regulatory environment, the OSINT toolset, or corporate law that has immediate implications for how our clients should approach their work.

Briefings are typically 2–4 pages. They do not hedge. They tell you what changed, why it matters to your practice, and what to do about it.

Access Policy
Briefings marked "Restricted" are available exclusively to active retainer clients (Firewall, Silent Partner). Briefings marked "Open" are available to all newsletter subscribers. To access restricted briefings, inquire about a retainer engagement via our client intake.
